SPRT Calculator

Sequential Probability Ratio Test — check results at any time without inflating false positives.

When to use: You want to monitor a running experiment and stop as soon as there is enough evidence — without the multiple-comparison penalty of peeking. Enter the cumulative totals as data accumulates. Re-run this calculator each time you check (daily, weekly) and follow the decision.

MDE (Minimum Detectable Effect) is the smallest absolute rate difference you care about detecting. Set it to the smallest lift that would be worth acting on.

Example: Day 14 of a sign-up rate test. 10,000 users per group so far. Control: 480 sign-ups. Treatment: 530. You want to detect any difference ≥ 1 percentage point (MDE = 0.01).
